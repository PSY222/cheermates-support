Frequently Asked Questions

What is Cheermates?

A shared to-do list for small circles — friends, roommates, couples. Everyone keeps their own list, and you cheer each other on to actually get things done.

How do I create or join a circle?

Open the app and tap "Create a circle" to start one, or "Join with an invite code" if a friend already sent you one.

How do I invite friends to my circle?

Tap the invite icon at the top of your Circle page, or the ⋮ menu next to a circle's name in My Circles, to share your invite link.

Are my private tasks visible to anyone else?

No. A task you mark private is only visible to you — everyone else in your circle just sees that you have one, not what it is.

How does Cheermates Pro work?

Pro is billed through the App Store and unlocks Insights, full task history, and joining more than 2 circles. Manage or cancel anytime from Settings → Manage Subscription, or from your iPhone's own Subscriptions settings.

How do I delete my account or data?

Email us at cheermates@creaitique.com and we'll delete your account and all associated data.

I found a bug, or have an idea for the app — who do I tell?

Email cheermates@creaitique.com. Bug reports, feature ideas, and general feedback are all welcome.
